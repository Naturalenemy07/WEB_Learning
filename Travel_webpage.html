About DC

Washington DC is the capital of the United States of America.

Getting Around

There are many options for getting around Washington DC. First, getting to DC is easy because there are 2 international airports within 15 minutes of DC. There is a major train station that offers rail transport along the Eastern seaboard. There are also numerous highways that lead to DC if you are driving!

Now that you have arrived in Washington DC, it is easy to get around the city! Taxis, Uber, Lyft are all methods of car transport but a cheaper option would be the amazing metro system. DC also has electric scooter and bicycles for rent throughout the city! There is always the option of walking, which I recommend if you are not travelling to far, this option will give you the opportunity to discover even more!
